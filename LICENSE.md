## Contribution Rule

By contributing, you agree that your contribution may be distributed under the
license category that matches the files you modify, unless the pull request
explicitly states a different compatible license and maintainers accept it.

Before a formal public standards release, maintainers should replace this
summary with full license texts or a REUSE-compatible license layout.
